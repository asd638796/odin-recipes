Shepherd's Pie Recipe

Description

Make this easy shepherd's pie recipe of a wintry evening, it's comfort food at its best. This one has a secret ingredient for extra savouriness. Don't worry about making it look fancy; the peaks and ridges on the mash just add to the appeal!

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz vegetable oil or beef dripping
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • etc...

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the vegetable oil. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large frying pan, heat the olive oil and fry the mince, stirring, until browned all over. While the meat is frying, break up any lumps with the back of the spoon.
  3. Stir the onions, then add the flour and stir. Add the bay leaves, thyme and anchovy and stir. Add the chopped tomatoes, stock (keep a little aside, for putting into the mince pan to de-glaze the pan) and Worcestershire sauce.
